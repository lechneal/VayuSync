Privacy Policy for VayuSync

VayuSync is designed with your privacy as a top priority.
- No Data Caollection: The app does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or files.
- Offline Functionality: All file operations (previewing, copying) happen exclusively on your device. The app does not require an internet connection and never sends your photos or videos to any server.
- Local Storage Only: The app only saves settings (like your chosen folders) and cached image previews on your device's local storage to improve performance. This data is never shared.

Your files are your files. Period.
